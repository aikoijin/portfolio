Hello there !

I'm Michelle, a Los Angeles based concept artist and illustrator, currently working on Godslayers at UC Santa Cruz!

I draw inspiration from Chinese architecture and patterns, techwear, and anime and semi-realism art styles. My goal is to make illustrations and character designs that connect with players around the world, using visual representation and design as a universal language.

I used to want to be a fashion designer, but I discovered a new passion for games after playing popular titles like League of Legends and Genshin Impact in 2017. I decided to combine my five years of fashion design experience with my love of gaming, and found the perfect balance in game art with a focus on character and concept art.
