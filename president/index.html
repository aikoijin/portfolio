Slug Gaming President Elections

Welcome

This is the official page of the 2024-2025 Slug Gaming Presidential Elections! As per the constitution, we are doing an all active officer election to decide our new president for the upcoming year. Vice president will be chosen by the new president. For those who have applied for president/are graduating this year, please do not fill out the form.


Please read over each applicant's blurb on how they plan on running the club next year and then click the button below to vote!

Serene Wong

For this next academic year, my plan would attempt to address a variety of problems that have been encountered. First of all, within the club, there is a lack of togetherness and cliques across both the general and officer body. To attempt to combat this I would continue with the monthly meetings/events that I hope would garner members together to create a greater sense of community. Additionally, potential check-ins and officer bonding time may produce a closer level of comradery that would then translate into the body. While it may not be ideal for some, establishing a standard for attendance and participation to ensure active involvement will also be a way to promote community amongst officers. Another way to approach the lack of togetherness would be integrating the competitive side of the club back into the community side as it has been seen to be mostly self-sustainable. Having the competitive parts not be as separated from the general community would allow for more fans to potentially develop and interact with those who represent our school, as well as bring a stronger sense of community that may help to revive certain communities within the club body. Of course, I would check in with each director to see what has and has not been working, but even with the integration, the competitive teams would be taken care of with the necessary resources and support to continue to thrive especially now that we have the space in Baskins. Continuing intramurals is also something that I would want to keep throughout the next year as it could bring a variety of people together. Next, would be addressing the perception of the club as a place for only hardcore gamers as well as being seen as a “boys club”. While that perception does hold in some areas of the club, I think that showcasing a diverse community would take a step toward a more inclusive environment, specifically through displaying a diverse body in the content we make as well as recognizing that this is a safe space. For example, I would continue with the variety style of content we have been recording to showcase the diverse officer body as it may then aid in promoting a wider, more inclusive audience. With the thought of content in mind, participating in trends such as TikToks may show our club to a wider audience with additional sharing across platforms. While I do think our presence on campus is lacking, continuing to promote both regular club activities and competitive events through socials, flyers, and other promotional material is the best that that could do thus far. Working with other clubs as well as the school to showcase our club is also something that may garner more attention, such as coordinating an event with SAMA or GDA. A different example would be working with on-campus artists to create an artist alley event where we could not only get some funding but also open our audience. For further recognition, it has been discussed with some other officers to coordinate a campus-wide gaming event similar to one held across the UCs to focus on collaboration with the community and competitive team, it would be held at each college with a variety of games to compete in and gain points for respective teams. Having smaller social events and fundraisers may additionally garner attention and funding, with an “open house” mindset for people to check out the club. Getting on top of fundraisers and having active participation is another thing I would attempt to address as there is a call for team funding but a lack of willingness to raise the money. Further with the thought of funding in mind producing things such as tote bags and other merchandise with a rebranded design of Slug Gaming is also something I plan to look into as it would get not only money but attention for our club. I hope to be able to work towards these goals the best that I can within the year, thank you for the consideration.
